Tokyo Revengers is an exhilarating manga and anime series that takes you on a thrilling journey through time travel and gang warfare. The story follows Takemichi Hanagaki, who discovers he has the ability to travel back in time to his middle school days. Determined to change the tragic fate of his friends and loved ones, Takemichi becomes embroiled in a dangerous battle against rival gangs.Tokyo Revengers offers a captivating and adrenaline-fueled experience for fans of crime dramas and time-travel narratives.
